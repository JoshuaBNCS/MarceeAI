About

Who's behind the workflows

I'm Joshua Novak, and I build the systems that quietly keep ecommerce businesses moving. For the past couple of years I've worked as an AI automation specialist and ecommerce analyst, and most days my job comes down to one thing: finding the repetitive, time draining work a team is stuck doing by hand and turning it into something that runs on its own. A lot of what I know is self taught, picked up the honest way, by building real things, breaking them, and staying with the problem until I understand why.

My range covers the whole pipeline. I design automations in n8n and Make, wire up AI agents that handle content and support, and connect the tools a business already lives in, from Gmail and Google Sheets to Shopify and Magento. I've built systems that generate SEO content at volume, pull order data straight out of an inbox, enrich messy product catalogs, and clean thousands of rows of data without anyone lifting a finger. Prompt engineering, data organization, and untangling workflow logic are where I feel most at home, and I'm comfortable owning a project from the first rough idea to something that runs reliably in production.

What I enjoy most is the puzzle of it. There's a specific kind of satisfaction in watching a process that used to eat someone's entire afternoon finish in a couple of minutes, and then do it again the next day without being asked. I care about building things that hold up, not just things that look good in a demo, so I test hard and keep refining until a workflow earns its place. If your team is losing hours to work a machine could be doing, that's exactly the kind of thing I'd love to help you hand off.
