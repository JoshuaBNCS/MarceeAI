About

Who's behind the workflows

Hey, I'm Joshua, the person behind MarceeAI. For the last couple of years I've been building automation systems for ecommerce businesses, and the short version is this: if a task is repetitive and eating up someone's day, I'm the one who builds the thing that takes it off their plate. Most of what I know came from just doing it, building workflows, watching them break, and figuring out how to make them run better.

I work mostly in n8n and Make, putting together the workflows and AI agents that quietly run in the background. I've built systems that write SEO content in bulk, pull order info straight out of an inbox, clean up thousands of messy spreadsheet rows, and handle the kind of busywork nobody actually wants to do. I'm comfortable across the whole thing, from the first rough idea to a workflow that's actually reliable and live, and I know my way around Shopify, Magento, Google Sheets, and prompt engineering well enough to connect all the pieces.

I'd rather build something that quietly works every day than something flashy that falls apart the second you look away.

Honestly, the part I like most is the problem solving. There's something satisfying about taking a process that used to eat a whole afternoon and getting it down to a couple of minutes that just runs on its own. I test everything hard before I call it done, and if your team is stuck doing work a machine could handle, that's exactly the kind of thing I love to take off your hands.
